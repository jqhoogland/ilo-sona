ma Nokewalana (toki Inli: North Carolina) li ma lili pi ma Mewika.

ma ni li lon poka pi ma Sakewalana li lon poka pi ma Sosa li lon poka pi ma Wesinja li lon poka pi ma Tenesi. ona li lon poka pi open suno pi ma Mewika li lon poka pi telo suli Alansi li lon selo ma.

ma lawa ona li ma tomo Wale. ma tomo Sale li suli nanpa wan.
