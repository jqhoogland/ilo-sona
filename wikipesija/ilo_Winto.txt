ilo Winto (toki Inli: Windows) li kulupu pi ilo kon lawa pi ilo sona. ona li pali tan kulupu Mikoso (Microsoft). jan li kepeken ilo Winto la, ona li kepeken supa pi sitelen kule. ilo Winto ante li lon ilo sona ante. ilo Winto nanpa wan li kama lon tenpo sike nanpa 1985. nanpa wan la, ilo Winto sin pi jan mute li ilo Winto nanpa 11 (Windows 11). ona li kama lon tenpo sike nanpa 2021.

ma ale la, ilo Winto li ilo kon lawa nanpa wan pi ilo sona supa.
