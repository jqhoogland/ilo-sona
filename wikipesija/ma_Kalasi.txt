ma Kalasi (toki Kalasi: Kalaallit Nunaat kalaːɬit nunaːt) anu ma Konlan (toki Tansi: Grønland ˈkʁɶnˌlænˀ) li ma lon telo suli Alansi lon ma Amelika lete. ma lon telo la, ona li suli nanpa wan. ma Kalasi li lon ma Tansi. ma Kalasi li ma pi lawa tu. 

toki lawa taso pi ma Kalasi li toki Kalasi. jan li kepeken toki Tansi kin.
