 ကမ္ဘာမကျေ (ma li lon la)  

ma Mijama (toki Mijama: မြန်မာ) li ma lon ma Asija. ma Mijama li lon poka pi ma Tawi  li lon poka pi ma Lajo  li lon poka pi ma Sonko  li lon poka pi ma Palata  li lon poka pi ma Panla.
