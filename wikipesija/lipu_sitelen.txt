toki musi (story) li musi lon toki. ona li ken kepeken e toki uta e sitelen toki e toki luka e nasin toki ante.

toki musi la, nasin tu suli li lon: toki pi lon ala (fiction) en toki lon (non-fiction).

sitelen pi toki musi (literature) li toki musi lon sitelen toki. ken la, sitelen li nasin pi awen toki musi. kulupu jan pi sitelen toki la, sitelen pi toki musi li kama suli mute tawa nasin jan. ona li suli tawa musi tawa sona tawa nasin sewi tawa nasin lawa. ona li nasin mute pi sitelen pi toki musi. nasin suli pi sitelen musi li sitelen pi kalama musi li sitelen toki pi pali musi li sitelen Posa (sitelen Posa li nasin ali ante pi sitelen toki musi).

tenpo pini mute la, jan mute li kepeken kiwen tawa sitelen e toki musi. tenpo ni la, jan mute li kepeken lipu toki anu ilo sona tawa sitelen e toki musi.
