ma Opeki (toki Opeki: Oʻzbekiston) anu ma Opekitan li ma lon ma Asija insa. ma Opeki li lon poka pi ma Tosiki  li lon poka pi ma Kilisa  li lon poka pi ma Kasatan  li lon poka pi ma Sikimen. li lon poka pi ma Akanisan.
