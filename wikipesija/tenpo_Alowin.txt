tenpo Alowin (toki Inli: Halloween) li tenpo suno musi lon ma mute. ona li lon tenpo suno nanpa 31 pi tenpo mun nanpa luka luka. tenpo suno kama pi tenpo Alowin li tenpo pi jan sewi ale. ni la, tenpo Alowin li jo kin e nimi ni: tenpo suno pini lon tenpo pi jan sewi ale (toki Inli: All Hallows' Eve (All Saints' Eve)).

jan li alasa monsuta e jan lon tenpo Alowin. tenpo pi pini suno la jan li tawa tomo mute ante. jan ni li toki e ni: "pakala anu suwi" (toki Inli: trick or treat). jan ni li wile kama jo e suwi. jan li kama jo ala e suwi la, jan li ken pana e pakala lili tawa tomo.
