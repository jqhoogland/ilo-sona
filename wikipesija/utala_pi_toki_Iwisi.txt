utala pi toki Iwisi li utala ni:

jan li sin e toki Iwisi li wile e ni: jan Juta mute li toki lon ona.

jan Juta mute li toki lon toki mute lon toki pi jan Juta.

jan pi utala toki li toki e ni: o toki taso lon toki Iwisi tawa jan lili sina! o pali ala e musi toki lon toki ante! toki Itesitosi li ike! o pali e ala lon ona!
