musi pi sike noka (toki Inli: Football) li musi sijelo li musi sike. jan li musi e ona lon ma ale. ona li musi utala nanpa wan lon ma ale. 

ona li kama tan ma Juke. ma ante li jo e musi pi nimi sama. taso, ma ante la, nimi pi musi ni li "musi Soka" (toki Inli: Soccer)

musi pi sike noka la, kulupu tu li lon. kulupu li alasa pana e sike lon lupa pi kulupu ante. jan li ken tawa e sike kepeken noka taso. jan li ken ala luka e sike.
