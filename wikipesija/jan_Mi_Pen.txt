 Mike Pence 

jan Mi Pen (toki Inli: Mike Pence) li jan lawa nanpa tu pi ma Mewika lon tenpo pini. tenpo lawa ona la jan lawa nanpa wan pi ma Mewika li jan Tana Tun. tenpo pini la jan Mi Pen li jan lawa kin pi ma Insijena.

ona li jan pi nasin sewi Kolisu. jan Mi Pen li pali e lipu lawa ike tawa jan kule.
