mani Seke sin (toki Iwisi: שקל חדש) li mani lon ma Isale. tenpo sike 1985 la, mani Seke sin li kama lon esun pi ma Isale. mani Seke pini li weka.

kipisi pi mani Seke sin li mani Akola. mani Seke sin wan li sama mani Akola ale.

lipu mani pi mani Seke sin mute li jo e sitelen pi jan Lake Peluten. lipu mani pi mani Seke sin mute mute luka luka li jo e sitelen pi jan Saju Jenekoki. lipu mani pi mani Seke sin ale li jo e sitelen pi jan Leja Kope. lipu mani pi mani Seke sin ale ale li jo e sitelen pi jan Natan Ateman.
