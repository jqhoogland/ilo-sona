jan Muwama, jan lili pi jan Apula (toki Alapi: مُحَمَّد ٱبْن عَبْد ٱللَّٰه (Muḥammad ibn ʿAbd Allāh)) li jan suli pi nasin sewi Isilan. jan pi nasin sewi li pilin e ni: ona li jan toki pini pi jan sewi. ona li kama lon tenpo sike 570 lon ma tomo Meka. ona li kama moli lon tenpo sike 632. ona li wan e ma palisa Alapi kepeken nasin sewi Silami.
