 Carolus Magnus 
jan Kalu suli (toki Lasina: Carolus Magnus) anu jan Saleman (Charlemagne) li jan lawa. ona li lawa e mute pi ma Elopa pi pini suno. mama pi ma Elopa la, jan li nimi e jan Saleman.

mama mije pi jan Saleman li jan Pepin lili. jan Kaloman nanpa wan li jan pi mama sama pi ma Saleman. jan Pepin li kama jan lawa pi kulupu Panki lon tenpo sike 751. tenpo sike 1768 la, jan Pepin li moli. tenpo sike 771 li Kaloman nanpa wan li moli kin. ni la, jan Saleman li kama jan lawa pi ma Panki ale.

jan Saleman li lanpan e ma mute lon ma Tosi lon ma Italija lon ma ante.

tenpo sike 1800 la, jan lawa sewi pi ma tomo Loma li toki e ni: jan Saleman li jan lawa suli pi ma Loma. ni li open pi ma suli Loma sewi.
