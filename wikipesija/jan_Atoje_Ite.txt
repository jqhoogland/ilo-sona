 Adolf Hitler 
jan Atoje Ite (toki Tosi: Adolf Hitler) li jan lawa pi ma Tosi lon 1933 tawa 1945. ona li lawa e ma Tosi tawa utala suli nanpa tu, li pali e nasin Nasi. ona li moli e jan Jejuta mute, e jan kule mute, e jan Loma mute, e jan mute tan kulupu mute. kulupu lawa pi ma ante li kama pakala e ona la, jan Atoje Ite li moli e ona.

lipu suli ona li lipu "utala mi" (toki Tosi: Mein Kampf).
