 Martin Luther King Junior 
jan Maten Luta Kin lili (toki Inli: Martin Luther King, Junior) li jan lawa pi nasin sewi Pasi li jan utala ala li jan lawa lon kulupu pimeja pi ma Mewika. tenpo pini moli la ona li jan lawa suli li nasin pi jan ken. ona li jan lawa kin pi kulupu sewi jan lawa pi nasin sewi Kolisu.

tenpo sike suno nanpa 1968 la jan alasa moli li moli e jan Maten Luta Kin lili. ni la utala suli li kama lon ma Mewika ale.
