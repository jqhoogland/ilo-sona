toki Awasa (toki Awasa: Harshe Hausa /ˈhaʊsə/) li toki suli lon ma Apika pi sunpo pini. kulupu jan Awasa li toki e ona. jan Awasa mute li lon ma Naselija sewi en ma Nise anpa. ona li lon ma Sate li lon ma Penin li lon ma Kamelun kin. ona li ken sitelen e toki kepeken sitelen Lasina anu sitelen Alapi.
