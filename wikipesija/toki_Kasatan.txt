 qazaqşaқазақша 
toki Kasatan (toki Kasatan: qazaqşa) li toki pi ma Kasatan. kin la, jan mute lon ma Kasatan li kepeken toki Losi.

toki Kasatan li toki pi ma mute. ona li toki lawa pi ma Kasatan li toki lon ma Sonko, lon ma Monko, lon ma Lusi, lon ma Kilisa, lon ma Opeki, lon ma mute ante. ante mute pi toki Kasatan li lon. sitelen la ona ale li sama mute. taso toki kalama la ona li ante mute.
