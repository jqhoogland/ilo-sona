kulupu jan Majoli (toki Majoli: Māori) li kulupu jan nanpa wan lon ma Nusilan. ona li kama tan ma Polinesija lon tenpo pini suli. tawa toki pi ma Nusilan la, ona li toki e ni: ma Otejalowa (Aotearoa). toki pi jan Majoli li toki Majoli.

pini e kama pi kulupu Majoli la, jan tan ma Elopa li kama. tawa toki pi jan Elopa la, jan Majoli li kepeken e nimi ni: jan Pakeja (Pākehā). ma Juke li kama lawa e ma Nusilan. taso, tenpo ni la, nasin pi jan Majoli li suli tawa jan pi ma Nusilan. jan Majoli mute li pilin pona tan e nasin ona.
