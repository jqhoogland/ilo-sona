 Barack Obama 
jan Sasa Opama


jan Pawa Usen Opama (toki Inli: Barack Hussein Obama bəˈrɑːk huːˈseɪn oʊˈbɑːmə) li jan lawa nanpa 44 pi ma Mewika. pini lon tenpo sike 2017 la, ona li kama jan lawa lon tenpo sike 2009. jan Sosi Waka Pusi li jan lawa lon tenpo pini. jan Tana Tun li jan lawa lon tenpo kama. jan lawa nanpa tu ona li jan So Piten.

tenpo suno 1961 lon ma tomo Onolulu pi ma Awawi. mama mije ona li kama lon ma Kenja. mama meli ona li kama lon ma tomo Wisito pi ma Kansa. ona li jan lawa pi ma Mewika pi mama pi ma Apika nanpa wan.
